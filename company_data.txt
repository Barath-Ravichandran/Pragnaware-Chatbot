Pragnaware Solutions Pvt Ltd is a Bengaluru-based company specializing in AI-driven, automation, and cybersecurity solutions. The company combines expertise in Artificial Intelligence (AI), Machine Learning (ML), Natural Language Processing (NLP), and digital security to build intelligent, scalable, and privacy-first systems.

Pragnaware serves industries including BFSI, Travel, Food & Hospitality, and Infrastructure & Construction. The focus is on delivering secure, innovative, and compliant digital transformation solutions.

---

Pragnaware Solutions delivers AI-driven and cybersecurity-focused digital solutions that help businesses innovate, scale, and stay protected. The company builds intelligent, secure, and privacy-first digital ecosystems.

---

Cybersecurity and AI are critical today. A cyberattack occurs every 39 seconds, the average data breach cost is $4.45 million, and over 80% of enterprises are adopting AI. Pragnaware helps businesses stay ahead with secure AI innovation.
